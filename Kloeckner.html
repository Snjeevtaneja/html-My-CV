Klockner Pentaplast (India) Pvt Ltd.

MD & CFO (Mar 07 - May10)

Key Achievements: Grew Revenue from USD 35 Mio to USD 135 Mio and EBIDTA from -7% to +22% in little over 2.5 years by:

  • Changing Business model - Launched new high margin products (Aclar, PET & Furniture Films), establishing Pan India Sales & Distribution network (reducing lead time from 6 weeks to 2 days) and effective supply chain Management. Introduced new grades in price sensitive categories to increase margin & capture bigger Market share.
  • Developed and authored Business plan and secured private equity Investment from Blackstone of Euro 22M to establish green field operations and Euro 13M from Consortium of Indian Banks.
  • Re-established financial controls, detailed product costing, financial modeling, accurate market mapping which resulted in change in Sales mix, increased market penetration, Reduction in Production Cost by 8% and Operational Cost by 15% and optimum utilization of resources leading the company from severe financial losses to strong and sustained bottom-line profitability in very 1st year.
  • Established new facility at Aurangabad and achieved reduction in production cost by 8% and operations cost by 15%.
