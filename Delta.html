Delta Energy Systems (India) Pvt. Ltd (Formerly known as Ascom India Pvt Ltd)

  • Funding work through IPO (complete Pre-Issue work issue size USD 180 Mio), External Commercial Borrowings and Bank Limits (Funds & Non-Funds based).
  • Mergers & Acquisitions to support aggressive in-organic growth strategy (Overall in-charge of 2 acquisitions (deal size of USD 65 M)).
  • Implementation of very strong MIS, Budgeting, Corporate governance & Internal Controls Mechanism.
  • Design & Implementation of ERP to provide highly integrated real time information system with stress on Risk Management and Corporate Governance.
  • Tax Planning & change in Org structure to maximize tax savings (+USD 0.5 Mio).
  • Cost reduction of over USD 3.7 Mio (reducing Inventory in WIP/ at site, relooking at sourcing and suppliers and devising an efficient distribution model).
